Our Programs for Kids

 

Little Bloom Playschool venture is into a complete preschooling sector for children in the age of 2 to 5 years. The Little Bloom playschool caters to the children in the toddler Playgroup, Pre-Nursery, Nursery, Kindergarten. These programs differ from level to level and program-based activities are conducted to develop the skills of children in the playgroup sector.
