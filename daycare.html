Day Care

 

Age Group:15 Months to 9 Years

   

We are a widely recognized play school that specializes in offering day service to your child after school. We are involved into rendering this service for a long time and have a vast experience. Under this service, we provide a wide range of facilities like safe, clean, hygienic & well equipped environment, safe drinking water, wholesome food and snacks prepared at home. Our day care service includes detailed report of breakfast, snacks, lunch, sleep, etc. All these reports are handed over to parents at the end of the day. Cultural activities and other festival celebrations also take place in our play school. Parents are contacted immediately while the child is administered first aid and taken to a nearby hospital with good medical facilities. The child is not left alone and there is no fear of abuse or neglect of your child as they are under the supervision of adults.
